# Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 16, 2026

Legal review required: this is an operating draft for a sensitive research collector, not legal advice.

## Short Version

- Open Dick Project collects volunteer-submitted, adult-only, self-shot private anatomy photos for segmentation research.
- Raw photos are not a public dataset and are not sold.
- Public outputs may include code, schemas, aggregate statistics, trained models, model cards, and non-sensitive examples.
- Contributors must not submit minors, third parties, faces, IDs, tattoos, documents, mirrors, or identifying background details.
- Contributors receive a deletion ID and can request deletion by emailing opendickpj@gmail.com.

## Data Collected

- Raw photo submitted through the collector.
- User-created or automatically drafted mask annotation.
- Consent flags: adult, own image, research use, no identifying details.
- Technical metadata for quality and research, such as image size, capture source, draft mask status, and image quality checks.
- Optional self-reported country or region, age, length, girth, and unit.
- Deletion ID.
- Limited page analytics. Raw images, mask geometry, deletion IDs, country, age, and size values must not be sent to analytics.

## Data Not Wanted

- Minor or suspected minor content.
- Third-party, scraped, stolen, leaked, coerced, revenge, or commercial adult content.
- Faces, ID cards, tattoos, documents, screens, mirrors, room numbers, addresses, usernames, or identifying backgrounds.
- Names, payment data, social profiles, or emails in the submission body.

## Use

Data is used to build and evaluate segmentation models, improve capture guidance and annotation workflows, produce aggregate research notes, process deletion requests, and protect the project from abuse.

## Sensitive Data

The data may reveal or imply sexual anatomy, sexual life, health-related details, or other sensitive personal information depending on the jurisdiction. Participation is voluntary.

## Storage and Sharing

Production submissions are stored in Cloudflare R2 under the Open Dick Project account. Raw images must not be committed to GitHub, placed in public website assets, published as a gallery, or sold. Public outputs should be derived and non-identifying.

## Retention and Deletion

Deletion requests remove the active raw image and metadata when a valid deletion ID is provided. Aggregated statistics or model artifacts already produced before deletion may not be practically reversible, but future training sets should exclude deleted IDs.
