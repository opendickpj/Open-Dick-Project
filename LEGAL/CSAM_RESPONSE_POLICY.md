# CSAM Response Policy

Last updated: June 16, 2026

Legal review required before broad public promotion.

## Zero Tolerance

Open Dick Project is 18+ only and does not knowingly accept, store, process, or distribute child sexual abuse material or sexual images of minors.

## Prohibited Content

- Any sexual image of a person under 18.
- Any image where age is unclear and the person may be under 18.
- Any third-party sexual image submitted without consent.
- Any attempt to use the project to store, route, test, classify, or distribute illegal sexual content.

## Operator Response

If suspected CSAM or minor sexual content is discovered:

- stop ordinary processing;
- do not publish, share, copy, or use the content for training;
- limit access to the minimum needed for legal and safety response;
- preserve only what counsel or applicable law requires;
- report to the relevant authority when legally required or appropriate, including NCMEC CyberTipline where applicable;
- delete or quarantine associated active objects according to legal advice and platform requirements.

## Safety Contact

Report safety concerns to opendickpj@gmail.com with the subject `Safety report`. Do not send copies of illegal content by email.
