## Purpose

Open Dick Project collects volunteer images and mask annotations to support private anatomy segmentation research. It is not a medical device, diagnostic tool, identity verification service, pornography hosting service, or commercial image marketplace.

## Eligibility

Contributors must:

- be 18 or older;
- submit only their own image;
- submit voluntarily;
- have legal capacity to consent;
- comply with applicable local law.

## Prohibited Submissions

- Any image of a minor or possible minor.
- Any third-party image without explicit consent.
- Scraped, stolen, leaked, coerced, revenge, or commercial adult content.
- Images containing faces, IDs, documents, tattoos, addresses, mirrors, usernames, or identifying background details.
- Illegal, exploitative, violent, or non-consensual content.
