# Consent Policy

Last updated: June 16, 2026

## Required Confirmations

Before submitting, contributors must confirm:

- I am 18 or older.
- This is my own image.
- I agree to private research use.
- No face or identifying details are included.

## Meaning of Consent

Consent means the contributor voluntarily submits a sensitive image and mask annotation for storage, review, processing, training, evaluation, safety review, and aggregate reporting by Open Dick Project.

Contributors should understand that sensitive anatomy images carry privacy risk even without a face.

## Optional Fields

Country or region, age, length, girth, and unit are optional. They may help aggregate research statistics but are not required.

## Not Consented

Consent does not permit:

- public raw-photo galleries;
- sale of raw photos;
- third-party images without consent;
- minor or suspected minor content.

## Withdrawal

Contributors can request deletion with their deletion ID. If the deletion ID is lost, the project may be unable to locate the submission without collecting additional identifying information, which the project tries to avoid.
